“Trader”, The Cryptocurrency Trading Platform

Project Description

“Trader”, from Improbability Labs Inc., is an innovative trading platform designed with a unique approach to algorithmic trading. Designed for all skill levels, “Trader” uses advanced algorithms to analyze trends and execute trades on major cryptocurrency exchanges, enhancing trading with sophisticated automation. Its intuitive features and comprehensive back-testing tools empower users to craft, assess, and perfect their strategies with historical data. A distinctive feature of “Trader” is its automated portfolio management, seamlessly integrating high-performing strategies and phasing out the less effective ones to optimize trading outcomes. By simplifying the complexities of the cryptocurrency market, “Trader” ensures an accessible and efficient trading experience, aimed at maximizing returns and minimizing risks.

Currently, users can tailor their trading approach by utilizing the Technical Analysis Trade Processor (TA-Bot) that supports long trades. This processor integrates various Technical Indicators via customizable algorithms, allowing users to refine their strategies. They can define parameters such as asset selection, exchange, profit-taking levels, stop-loss settings, buy size, the duration of open trades, along with their choice of algorithm to match their specific needs. While the current focus is on the TA-Bot Processor, it's noteworthy that this platform is designed to easily accommodate additional processors, such as Grid bots, DCA (Dollar Cost Averaging) bots, or specific limit/market trade functionalities, aligning with different trading strategies in the future.

Algorithms play a crucial role in the functionality of processors like the TA-Bot Processor. They empower users to enhance their trading strategies by incorporating Technical Indicators such as RSI (Relative Strength Index), MACD (Moving Average Convergence Divergence), ADX (Average Directional Index), Bollinger Bands (BB), and many more. Users can input these indicators into the trading system's historical data to formulate specific buy and sell rules. By defining the parameters of these indicators in conjunction with the Open, High, Low, Close, and Volume (OHLCV) data, users can create nuanced trading strategies.

Back testing serves as a pivotal tool in the trading system. After users have crafted their algorithms or chosen to explore default ones, they can engage in back testing to evaluate their effectiveness. This process allows for testing across various crypto exchanges and crypto currency assets, over different time periods, such as 3 minutes, 4 hours, and everything in between. During back testing, users can adjust parameters like stop-loss values, profit-take levels, and maximum open period durations to simulate real trading conditions. This comprehensive testing phase helps users to identify and refine the most promising strategies, which they can then confidently implement in live trading.

In live trading, users can meticulously customize their strategies just like they did during the back testing phase. Once set up, the system executes these strategies around the clock, allowing users to run multiple trade strategies simultaneously. Live mode offers real-time monitoring, enabling traders to get a clear picture of their strategies effectiveness, detailed by metrics that highlight performance trends and trading activity status.

The “Trader” system provides an advanced feature to enhance risk management. It can automatically deactivate strategies based on predefined criteria, enhancing the safety and adaptability of trading operations. For example, the TA-Bot Processor might disable a strategy if it detects unfavorable trends such as a deteriorating win/loss ratio, a series of consecutive losses, or significant changes in market indicators like the Order Book Imbalance (OBI). This functionality ensures that trading activities are dynamically adjusted to maintain alignment with market conditions and the trader’s risk tolerance.

The trading system also incorporates an advanced automatic trade finder function. This tool utilizes the users’ strategies, back testing them across all assets and time periods to unearth optimal trading opportunities. Given its intensive computational requirements, this function is usually run on a weekly basis. After the analysis, the system can automatically and seamlessly incorporate the best preforming strategies into the user’s live trading setup.

The evaluation of these opportunities is based on various performance metrics, such as the win/loss ratio, Sharpe ratio, the time elapsed since the last trade, and others, ensuring that the selected strategies are not only theoretically sound but also practical and relevant to the current market dynamics. This approach, focusing on recent back testing periods, aims to capture and capitalize on the prevailing market conditions, enhancing the responsiveness and effectiveness of the trading system.

“Trader” by Improbability Labs Inc. stands as a unique platform in the realm of algorithmic trading, offering a dynamic and user-friendly experience that caters to both novice and experienced traders. Its intelligent integration of automated portfolio management and advanced analytical tools, including the TA-Bot Processor, facilitates a nuanced and proactive trading environment. This environment allows users to navigate the complexities of the cryptocurrency market with greater confidence and precision. By leveraging state-of-the-art algorithms and comprehensive back-testing capabilities, “Trader” equips users with the means to optimize their trading strategies, adapt to market changes, and achieve superior financial outcomes. As the platform continues to evolve, incorporating additional processors and functionalities, “Trader” aims to set new standards in trading technology, driving the future of investment and wealth generation in the digital age.

Current Technical Stack

The “Trader” system is underpinned by a robust and sophisticated technical architecture designed to ensure scalability, reliability, and high performance. Below are the key components of our technical stack:

  • Slurm HPC Cluster: A powerful and flexible high-performance computing (HPC) cluster management system, enabling efficient scheduling and job management for scalable computing resources.
  • Proxmox/KVM Hypervisor: Utilizing the Proxmox Virtual Environment with KVM (Kernel-based Virtual Machine) hypervisor technology, ensuring secure and isolated virtualization capabilities for managing and running virtual appliances and machines.
  • Ubuntu 22.04: A stable and secure Linux distribution, Ubuntu 22.04 provides a reliable foundation with long-term support, ensuring consistent operation and compatibility.
  • MySQL Cluster (Percona 8.0): An enterprise-grade, high-availability MySQL cluster solution, using Percona Server 8.0, offers enhanced performance and scalability for handling extensive datasets with high transaction rates.
  • Grafana: A leading open-source platform for monitoring and observability, Grafana allows for real-time visualization of metrics and analytics, aiding in insightful decision-making.
  • Python (Pandas/TA-Lib/Numpy/Flask): Our data science stack incorporates Python with key libraries such as Pandas for data manipulation, TALIB for technical analysis, and NumPy for numerical computations, forming a comprehensive toolkit for advanced data analysis.
  • Bash: A versatile command-line interface and scripting language, Bash enables efficient system control and automation, enhancing our operational capabilities and process workflows.

This technical infrastructure is designed to support and enhance the system's functionality, providing a resilient and high-performing environment for our users and their trading activities.

Additional Prototypes and Innovations

In our continuous pursuit of excellence and innovation, Improbability Labs has developed and tested several prototypes alongside our main cryptocurrency trading platform. One notable project is a trading system that integrates with the Trader Workstation (TWS) API from Interactive Brokers and the Alpha Vantage API. This system is capable of executing trades during regular trading hours, leveraging the comprehensive financial data and trading capabilities of these platforms. However, due to the complexity of maintaining a local instance of TWS in sync with our server, this prototype, despite being fully functional, may not be continued.

Furthermore, we have explored the integration of advanced AI algorithms into our trading systems. One pioneering development is an algorithm that interfaces with OpenAI's GPT models, utilizing it to analyze current Open, High, Low, Close, and Volume (OHLCV) data along with technical indicators. This AI-driven approach helps in making informed decisions about whether to buy or sell, as well as in predicting optimal stop-loss and profit-taking levels. While promising, the integration of this advanced AI functionality into our primary cryptocurrency trading platform is planned, pending allocation of a development budget for extensive testing. This feature is designed to be compatible not only with OpenAI's models but also with other AI models, ensuring flexibility and adaptability to a range of analytical needs.

These initiatives reflect our commitment to enhance trading outcomes and continuously improving the platform. Although some of these prototypes may not advance to full-scale development, they play a crucial role in our ongoing research and innovation efforts, helping us to refine our understanding of what works best in dynamic trading environments.

Development Roadmap and Future Enhancements

Our development strategy encompasses several key initiatives aimed at enhancing the platform's functionality, scalability, and user experience. The following steps outline our focused approach to advancing the system:

  • API Containerization: Convert the existing Python 3 APIs into Docker files to facilitate easier deployment, version control, and environment consistency across development and production stages.
  • Scalability with Kubernetes: Develop Kubernetes manifests to enable efficient production scaling and hosting, ensuring that our system can dynamically adjust to varying load and performance requirements.
  • Code Quality Improvements: Enhance the codebase by adding comprehensive comments and documentation. This will not only improve maintainability and ease of understanding for new developers but also ensure that future modifications are conducted systematically.
  • User and Developer Documentation: While user documentation is dependent on the completion of a front-end interface, initial efforts should focus on developing robust developer documentation to streamline future development and onboarding processes.
  • Geographic Redeployment: Given the stringent cryptocurrency regulations in Canada, plan for redeployment of the system to a jurisdiction with more favorable cryptocurrency trading laws to avoid operational hindrances.
  • Database Integration for Algorithms: Transition from file-based to database-driven storage for algorithms, enabling users to design and modify their trading strategies directly through a web interface.
  • Trade Processor Enhancements: Expand the variety of trade processors to include different order types, grid bots, DCA bots, and AI-driven processors, thus broadening the trading capabilities and options available to users.
  • Front-End Development: Design and implement a user-friendly front-end interface that includes features like authentication, subscription management, strategy creation, strategy back testing, and algorithm configuration. This interface is crucial for market entry, as it will significantly enhance user interaction and operational efficiency.
